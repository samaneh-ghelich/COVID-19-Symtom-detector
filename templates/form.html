Purpose

This form is to help planning for the population of a region. It is not for providing individual advice. It does not replace NHS 111 or 999. If you want individual advice please use the 111 Online form.

This service does not replace diagnostic testing.

By allowing people to self report symptoms and diagnosis of COVID-19 our researchers will build up a better picture of the number of people who have contracted the COVID-19 virus and use this information to plan regional services. We may also use this information for ethically approved research for the NHS. Further details about how we will use this data are available in the data processing policy.
